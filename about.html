About Us

The Galactic Empire is committed to maintaining peace and order throughout the galaxy. At it's core, it holds the wants and needs of its citizens. A citizen of the Empire is a happy citizen.

Vader is a ruthless leader. He goes where he is needed and ensures he is not needed too long. Wherever Lord Vader travels, the Empire's peace and security is sure to follow.
Palpatine is our glorious leader. Through his extreme philanthropy, the Empire remains stable. He is the one solely responsible for bringing the government back up from the ashes of the Clone Wars.
Admiral Tarkin is the highest authority on the Death Star. Spending endless hours, determining which of the Empire's holding's need him most, he selflessly makes the decisions that put the Empire's most powerful tool into action.
Commander Veers is in charge of the Imperial ground forces. Utilizing state of the art technology, such as the AT-AT Walker, Veers goes to the most dangerous parts of the galaxy to secure the peace that the Empire so values.
This is TK421, a stormtrooper who works on the Death Star. His leadership has inspired many and he is revered by his fellow Stormtroopers as a irreplacable member of the team.
Boba Fett is an incredible asset to the empire. As freelance bounty hunter, the Empire gives him bounties on the heads of various Rebels. He helps keep the galaxy clean so you can fly safe.
